# About Jillaine

## Who She Is

Jillaine is a tattoo artist — and one of the **top tattoo cover-up artists in the world**.

## What She Creates

Her specialty is **colour realism**. She creates:

- Beautiful, vibrant **nature landscapes**
- **Northern lights**
- **Space** scenes
- **Animals**
- Stunning **cover-ups** that transform old or unwanted tattoos into breathtaking new pieces

## Her Signature

Vivid colour, incredible realism, and the ability to turn skin into a canvas that holds the awe of the natural world.
